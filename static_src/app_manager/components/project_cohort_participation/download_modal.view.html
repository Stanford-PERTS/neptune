What Am I Downloading?

A CSV file—which you can open in a spreadsheet—of identifiers of participants who participated in one or more modules in the date range {[ startDate | date:'shortDate' ]} - {[ endDate | date:'shortDate' ]}.

The column token will contain the identifier. The identifier will be whatever the participant entered when they began the module (e.g. their name or student ID) or what your custom portal sent to our system.

The column percent_progress will tell you what percent of the module the student completed.

  • 1: The participant entered the module
  • 33: The participant has completed pre-program questions
  • 66: The participant has completed the pre-program questions and the program.
  • 100: The participant has completed the program in its entirety (pre- and post-program questions)

The column module will tell you which module they completed. This is most relevant to programs with more than one session.

These data contain individual student identifiers. Handle them with care!
